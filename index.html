Welcome to Systems in Play!


This is a video game podcast where new game devs Sam and Cj discuss games and their mechanics that inspire us. Join us every two weeks for a new discussion topic as well as some chatting about the games we've been playing, new releases, and our current projects.
Our theme song was written by Cj, and our cover art was drawn by Sam.

For any questions, please contact us by sending an email to systemsinplaypod@gmail.com.
